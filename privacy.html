Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-08-29

This Chrome Extension uses the Pinterest API to create Pins on your behalf.

Data We Access

  • Pinterest account scopes strictly for: reading your boards and creating Pins.
  • Page data from the active Adobe Stock tab (title, image URL, page URL) to pre-fill a Pin.

How We Use Your Data

  • Tokens are stored locally in your browser via chrome.storage.
  • No personal data or tokens are sold, shared, or stored on external servers.
  • You can revoke access anytime from your Pinterest account settings.

Third Parties

We interact with Pinterest’s API and the Adobe Stock webpage you visit to extract metadata for pin creation.
